SATIONARY

A time series is stationay if it is a statistical properties(meaan,variance,autocorelation)reamins constant over time

-HYPOTHESIS OD ADF TEST 1.NULL HYPOTHESIS:-The time series has a unit root(i.e,it is a non-satiionary) 2.ALTERNATIVE HYPOTHESIS:-The time series does not have any unit root(i.e,it is stationary)

INTERPRETING ADF TEST RESULTS: 1.if the p-value is less than 0.05,reject Ho ->The series is stationary 2.if the p-value is more than 0.05,fail to reject Ho->The series is non-stationary

SEASONAL DECOMPOSITION

USE SEASONAL DECOMPOSITION TO ANALYZE THE TREND SEASONALITY AND RESIDUALS

TREND:-the long term pattern(increase or decrease overtime SEASONALITY:-The repeating patterns at fuxed intervals (example:-monthly sales spikes) RESIDUAL:-The random variations that re not explained by trend or seasonality

INTEPRETING THE OUTPUT: 1.Original series::-The raw time series data 2. Trend Component:-The genral direction of the data over time 3.Seasonal component:-The repeating patterns(example:-higher sales in december) 4.Residual component:-The remaining part after remiving trend and seasonality(example:-random noise)
